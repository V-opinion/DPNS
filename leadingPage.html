Frequently Asked Questions

What is Flaggdesign?

Flaggdesign is a new flag design concept that seeks to remove religious and hard-to-interpret symbols from flags and instead represent everyone regardless of their faith or beliefs. We believe that religion should be separated from politics or sports, and we strive to create flags that reflect this belief. Our mission is to create a unified flag design that unites people while respecting individual religions.

Är Flaggdesign en politisk organisation?

Nej, Flaggdesign har ingenting att göra med politiska organisationer eller agenda. Vi är helt oberoende och vårt mål är att skapa en flaggdesign som representerar alla, oavsett religion eller bakgrund. Vi strävar efter att ge en vacker design som sammanfogar alla, så att man kan stolt bära den på sina föremål.

What is the difference between a flag and a banner?

While both flags and banners are ways of expressing ideas or beliefs, there is an important distinction between the two. A flag is usually used as a symbol or representation of a country, group, or organization. It is often flown from poles or from buildings and tends to be square or rectangular in shape. Banners on the other hand, tend to be larger and more flexible in size and design. They are often used for short-term campaigns, events, or other messages that may not require a physical flagpole.

What is the meaning of the flaggdesign?

The flaggdesign is a representation of our commitment to inclusivity and unity. It is a modern way of showcasing our shared values, while still respecting the individuality and beliefs of each person. The design focuses on the core principles that create a strong community, such as respect for diversity, freedom of expression, and acceptance of all people regardless of their background or beliefs. We hope it serves as a reminder to everyone that everyone is welcome here and that we value everyone's contributions to society.

Is the new flag design only for religious purposes?

Absolutely not! Our flag design is meant to be inclusive and not exclusive. We are creating a design that will represent all people, regardless of their religion or beliefs. We believe in keeping religion separate from sports and politics, which is why our flag design does not include religious symbols or difficult-to-interpret imagery.
